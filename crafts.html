Traditional Crafts

Basotho pottery is made using traditional techniques passed down through generations. These clay vessels are often decorated with symbolic patterns a nd used in daily life ceremonies.

Beadwork is a vibrant expression of Basotho identity.Patterns and colors often signify social status,marital status ,or clan affiliation.

Moshoeshoe Day commemorates King Moshoeshoe I,the founder of the Basotho nation.It is market by parades,speeches and traditional performance across Lesotho.
