Basotho cultural heritage

WELCOME CULTURAL HERITAGE

Basotho heritage is the cultural identity of the Basotho people of Lesotho, rooted in their history,traditions, and way of life.It includes the Sesotho language, traditional dress like the SEANAMARENA blanket and MOKOROTLO hat,and customs duch as respect for elders and communal living.Music,dance,poetry, and oral storytelling play a big role in preserving their history. Important historical sites like Thaba-Bosiu remind Basotho of thier fight for unityand independance.
